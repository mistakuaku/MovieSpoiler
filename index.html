The Matrix

Released: March 31, 1999 (USA)

Starring

  • Keanu Reeves
  • Laurence Fishburne
  • Carrie-Anne Moss
  • Hugo Weaving

Movie Quote

"There is no spoon."

Plot

The story revolves around Neo (Keanu Reeves) a computer hacker who on his search for a legendary hacker Morpheus (Laurence Fishburne) finds out that his life up until now has been a lie. Everything he knew and understood is all a simulation created by sentient robots who use humans as a source of power to keep themselves alive. To find out why Neo, Morpheus, and their rag tag gang of hackers go on a journey to discover the truth fighting the robots along the way. [Source]
